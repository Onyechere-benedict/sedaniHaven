Welcome to Sedani Haven

Sedani Haven is a premier shortlet apartment provider in Port Harcourt, Nigeria. We offer luxurious and comfortable accommodations for both business and leisure travelers seeking a home away from home.

Our apartments are thoughtfully designed with modern amenities and elegant furnishings to ensure a memorable stay. Whether you're visiting for a day or an extended period, Sedani Haven is your perfect choice for accommodation in Port Harcourt.

At Sedani Haven, we believe in providing exceptional service and creating memorable experiences for our guests. Our dedicated team is committed to ensuring your comfort and satisfaction throughout your stay.

Our Mission

Our mission at Sedani Haven is to provide exceptional accommodation experiences that exceed our guests' expectations. We strive to create a comfortable, luxurious, and welcoming environment where guests can relax and feel at home.

We are committed to delivering personalized service, maintaining high standards of cleanliness and comfort, and ensuring that every aspect of our guests' stay is memorable for all the right reasons.
